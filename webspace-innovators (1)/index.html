About ProBuildManager

ProBuildManager is a comprehensive construction management system designed to streamline project management, enhance resource allocation, provide robust record-keeping, and generate effective reports for ThobileM Construction.

About ThobileM Construction

  • Founded: November 2019
  • Location: Roodepoort, Gauteng
  • Specialties: School construction, water pipelines, roads
  • Reputation: Known for delivering high-quality infrastructure

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

RESOURCE MANAGEMENT

Generate detailed reports for better decision-making and insights
